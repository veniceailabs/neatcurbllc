HERE'S WHAT YOU NEED TO DO (2 STEPS):

STEP 1: Log Into Your Admin Dashboard
───────────────────────────────────────
Go to: https://neatcurbllc.com/admin


STEP 2: Click "Import Data" in the Left Sidebar
────────────────────────────────────────────────
You'll see a button that says:

    🚀 Import All Yardbook Data

Click it. That's it!


WHAT HAPPENS NEXT:
──────────────────
✅ Your customers will appear in "Clients"
✅ Your invoices will be imported
✅ Your payments will be imported
✅ Your expenses will appear in "Expenses"

You'll see how many records were imported.


VERIFY YOUR DATA:
─────────────────
After importing, check:
  • Go to "Clients" → See your customers
  • Go to "Expenses" → See your expense totals
  • Check that amounts look correct


THAT'S IT! 🎉
──────────────
Your Yardbook data is now in Neat Curb.
You have all the same features plus MORE.


WHAT IF SOMETHING GOES WRONG?
──────────────────────────────
The import is automatic. If it fails:
  1. Try again - sometimes it's just slow
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. If it still doesn't work, let us know


QUESTIONS?
───────────
Everything you need to know is in the dashboard.
The import is designed to be super simple.

Just click the button and let it do its thing!
