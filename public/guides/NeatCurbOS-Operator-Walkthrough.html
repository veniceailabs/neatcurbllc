NeatCurbOS Module (Embedded in Dashboard)

How to Use Each Card

This module lives inside Dashboard and is your main control board.

Pillar What It Means What To Click
Fleet & Rig Management See active and queued work so crews stay moving. Open /admin/jobs and /admin/work-orders
Instant Quoting Create fast, margin-aware estimates from lead details. Open /admin/lead-intake then confirm in /admin/leads
Smart Scheduling Move jobs from queued to in-progress to complete. Use /admin/jobs and crew updates in /admin/work-orders
Customer & Lead CRM Track leads, converted clients, and communications. Use /admin/clients + /admin/messages
Financial Visibility Watch invoice pipeline and paid revenue totals. Review /admin/invoices + /admin/audit
AI Assistant Use Business AI for quick navigation and ops prompts. Click Open NeatCurbOS or Open Business AI from Dashboard.
Tip: If you are unsure where to go, start at Dashboard → NeatCurbOS card → click a shortcut.
Daily Operations

Simple Operating Rhythm

Morning (15 min)

  • Open Dashboard.
  • Check active and queued jobs.
  • Review new leads.
  • Send first follow-ups.

Midday (10 min)

  • Update job statuses.
  • Convert hot leads.
  • Confirm crew ETAs.
  • Reply to customer messages.

End of Day (20 min)

  • Close completed jobs.
  • Upload proof in work orders.
  • Check invoice status.
  • Review audit log quickly.
Lead Status: new → contacted → converted Job Status: queued → in_progress → complete Invoices: draft/sent need follow-up
Storm-Day Playbook

When Snow Hits

  1. Open /admin and click Activate Snow Ready.
  2. Go to /admin/jobs and confirm queued snow jobs.
  3. Use /admin/work-orders to track crew start/finish.
  4. Send updates from /admin/messages to priority clients.
  5. At day end, close completed jobs and check /admin/audit.

Troubleshooting

If Something Looks Wrong

Cannot log in

  • Use /admin/login.
  • Use Send Magic Sign-In Link first.
  • If needed, use Owner Account Rescue (for owner emails).
  • If still needed, use password reset.
  • Try a private browser window.

No data on dashboard

  • Refresh browser once.
  • Check internet is stable.
  • Check /admin/audit for recent activity.

Messages not sending

  • Confirm client phone/email exists.
  • Try the other channel (SMS vs email).
  • Re-open Messages page and resend.

Jobs not updating

  • Confirm job was saved in Jobs page.
  • Check staff view in Work Orders.
  • Verify status values are valid.
Rule of thumb: If unsure, go back to Dashboard and use the NeatCurbOS card shortcuts.
